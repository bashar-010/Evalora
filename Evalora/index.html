About Evalora

Evalora is an AI-powered networking platform designed for university students.
It helps students showcase their skills, projects, and achievements, while connecting them with mentors, peers, and real-world opportunities - all through a smart dynamic scoring system.

How The AI Calculate Your Score

Skills Analysis

AI checks the number and variety of skills and how well they match your field.

Project Impact

Your completed projects and their professional impact raise your score.

Engagement & Collaboration

The more you join groups, help others, and participate, the higher your engagement score.

Achievements & Endorsements

Endorsements, volunteering, and peer recommendations boost your overall rating.
